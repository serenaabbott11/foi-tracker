Prototype → production

  • When going from prototype to production, it's important to find resources that will be useful — and to not trust Claude when dealing with business rules around regulations.
  • Example: finding a public API, like the bank holidays one.
  • When asking Claude, it makes assumptions — for example only choosing the England & Wales bank holidays, when the regulations include all four nations.
  • Trusting Claude without doing proper research and checks can mean breaking regulations.
  • Particularly important for government projects.
